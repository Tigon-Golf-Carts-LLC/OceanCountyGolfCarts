### Industry Terminology Definitions

**Street Legal**: Golf carts equipped with safety features (headlights, turn signals, mirrors, seat belts) and registered with the DMV for road use.

**LSV (Low Speed Vehicle)**: Vehicles designed to operate at speeds between 20-25 mph on roads with speed limits of 35 mph or less.

**Range**: Distance a golf cart can travel on a single battery charge under normal operating conditions.

**Payload**: Maximum weight capacity including passengers and cargo.

**Lithium-Ion**: Advanced battery technology offering longer life, faster charging, and consistent power delivery compared to lead-acid batteries.
