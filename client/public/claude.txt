A: The main differences are design focus and capabilities. The EV CITY is optimized for urban use with standard seating and street-legal features for neighborhood transportation. The EV NOMAD adds all-terrain tires and lifted suspension, making it better suited for rougher terrain and outdoor adventures. Both share the same 72V lithium battery and 5KW motor, providing 25 mph top speed and 40-50 mile range.

Q: Can I drive a golf cart on Ocean County roads?
A: Yes, but it depends on the specific model and local regulations. DENAGO models are street-legal when properly equipped with safety features like headlights, turn signals, mirrors, and seat belts. They can operate on roads with speed limits of 35 mph or less. EVOLUTION models are typically designed for golf course and private property use. We help customers understand local requirements and ensure proper registration when needed.
